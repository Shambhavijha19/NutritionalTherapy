Our Services

Comprehensive nutritional support at every stage of your health journey.

Nutritional Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your current health status, dietary habits, and nutritional needs to create the perfect starting point for your wellness journey.

Our nutritional assessment includes:

  • Detailed dietary history
  • Analysis of current eating patterns
  • Evaluation of nutritional deficiencies
  • Body composition analysis
  • Health goals discussion

Personalized Meal Planning

Custom meal plans designed specifically for your nutritional needs, food preferences, and lifestyle to make healthy eating simple and enjoyable.

Our meal planning services include:

  • Daily or weekly meal schedules
  • Portion size recommendations
  • Grocery shopping lists
  • Recipe suggestions and alternatives
  • Consideration of food allergies

Therapeutic Diets

Specialized nutrition plans for managing specific health conditions and promoting healing through evidence-based dietary interventions.

We offer therapeutic diets for:

  • Diabetes management
  • Heart disease & hypertension
  • Digestive disorders (IBS, GERD)
  • Food allergies & intolerances
  • Weight management
  • Autoimmune conditions

Nutritional Counseling

One-on-one guidance to help you understand nutrition principles, build sustainable habits, and make lasting changes to your relationship with food.

Our counseling approach includes:

  • Education on nutrition fundamentals
  • Behavior change strategies
  • Emotional aspects of eating
  • Practical tips for healthy eating
  • Support for overcoming challenges

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first consultation?

During your first consultation, we will discuss your health history, current diet, lifestyle, and health goals. I will perform a nutritional assessment and create a personalized plan tailored to your specific needs. The initial consultation typically lasts about 60–90 minutes.

How often will I need to schedule appointments?

After the initial consultation, follow-up appointments are typically scheduled every 2–4 weeks depending on your specific needs and goals. As you progress, the frequency may decrease to monthly or quarterly check-ins for maintenance.

Can you help with specific health conditions?

Yes, I specialize in creating therapeutic diets for various health conditions including diabetes, hypertension, digestive disorders (IBS, GERD, etc.), weight management, hormonal imbalances, and more. Each plan is customized to address your specific health concerns.

Do you provide meal plans?

Yes, I provide detailed meal plans tailored to your preferences, lifestyle, and nutritional needs. These can be daily or weekly plans with recipes and shopping lists to make implementation easier.

How long will it take to see results?

Results vary depending on your specific goals and health condition. Some clients notice improvements in energy and digestion within a few weeks, while other changes like weight loss or managing chronic conditions may take longer. We'll set realistic expectations during your consultation.
